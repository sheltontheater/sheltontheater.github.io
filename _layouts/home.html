Visit Us

The Shelton Theater is a non-profit organization that was founded in 1993. The Shelton family and its successors have been creating, supporting, and nurturing artists in the Bay Area since 1961. This lively organization with its rich history and powerful role in the arts community produces and hosts stand-up and improvisational comedy. It also shows artwork by local and guest artists. Until 2020, it produced plays, musicals, stand up comedy, music events, cabaret shows, film screenings, and lit slams.

All donations are tax deductible. We are a welcoming community.
