description: Frequently asked questions about the Shelton Theater
---

<details markdown="1">
<summary>When does the box office open for my show?</summary>

1 hour before showtime.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>When does the house open?</summary>

5-10 minutes before showtime.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How is seating arranged?</summary>

General admission: first come first served upon house opening.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Are there concessions?</summary>

Full bar opens 1 hour before showtime and 2 hours afterwards or until 2:00 AM, whichever is earlier.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Is there elevator access and ADA seating?</summary>

Yes, please notify the producers of the show you plan to attend in advance so that they can accommodate you to the best of their ability.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Do you seat latecomers?</summary>

Seating is allowed until 20-30 minutes after showtime, depending on the show.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Where can I park?</summary>

We encourage attendees to use public transit; but for those who are driving, the [Sutter-Stockton Garage](https://www.sfmta.com/garages-lots/sutter-stockton-garage) is nearby and reasonably priced. We do not recommend parking on the street.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Do you take credit cards?</summary>

Yes, all major credit cards are accepted.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Where are the restrooms?</summary>

2 on the ground floor at the end of the hallway and 2 on the second floor.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Can I rent the venue for a private event?</summary>

Yes, please visit our [Venue Rental](/venue-rental/) page for more details.

</details>
